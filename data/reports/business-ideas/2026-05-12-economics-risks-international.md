## Key strategic insights from this sweep

### Wildfire insurance is the deal-killer hidden in 10+ live Ideas
Castle Rock Springs, Pine Grove Resort, Snowshoe Brewery, Kehret Vineyards, Lone Buffalo, Georgetown Distillery, Eden Vale Inn, Italian Bar Rd, AVBC, El Dorado vet — every one carries CA wildfire insurance retreat risk. State Farm + Allstate + Farmers have stopped writing in fire-prone zones. **Action: get insurance quotes BEFORE LOI on any of these, and make insurance a deal condition.**

### Spain Golden Visa ENDED April 3, 2025
Critical update for international thesis. RE acquisition alone NO LONGER triggers Spanish residency. Must pursue Self-Employed Work Visa (operator route) OR Non-Lucrative Visa (passive income test). For La Rioja Hotel + Galicia rectory: operator route is the cleaner path; structure Spanish entity (SL) at LOI.

### Wolf Camp is the closest-to-thesis youth ed find
30-year nature/wilderness school with active waitlist > capacity. Sellers explicitly invite multi-year transition with 1-10yr earnout (5yr suggested). **No real-estate carry** — operates at permitted public/leased venues. Capital-light model means high SDE-to-purchase ratio. Bay Area is DRY for nature schools (Trackers, Berkeley Forest School all founder-operated, none signaling exit).

### Pet care recurring revenue is the underrated category
3 strong fits in the pet care sweep:
- **El Dorado Vet (CAV3023)** — Caffe Trieste model applied to vet care, $900k + RE option, 1+ acre expansion room. Lifestyle 3/3, recession-resistant cash-pay revenue.
- **Contra Costa Pet Grooming+Boarding (1983)** — 42-year operating history. Multi-service stack (grooming + boarding + cat). Free-standing building = potential RE purchase optionality.
- **NorCal Dog Training (SBA absentee)** — $599k, $220k SDE, lowest-risk first-acquisition candidate.

### Mexico Bacalar is the cleanest international find
24-room boutique on 10 hectares lakefront. Bacalar is INLAND 30km from coast = fee-simple OK (not ejido land + not within 50km coastal restriction zone). Caribbean lakefront RE moat + multi-line ops + US/Canada eco-luxury target demographic. Visa path via RT/RP (cleanest of new 3 countries).

### Italy ERV is the cleanest of the 3 new EU residency paths
Italian Elective Residency Visa requires €32k passive income + housing (which RE satisfies). Cleaner than Spain post-Golden-Visa or Portugal's tightening D7 clock. Montalcino (Brunello DOCG) and Siena 14-bedroom both qualify. Investor Visa via €500k into the Srl is alternative pathway.

### Veterinary practices have a deal-killer-grade risk
Non-DVM buyer MUST retain DVM as medical director OR have DVM partner. Veterinary Medicine Board + DEA registration transfer cannot complete without DVM continuity. El Dorado + Sonoma vet listings carry this risk. **Plan: identify DVM partner pre-LOI, or skip the category.**
