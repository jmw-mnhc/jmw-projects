## 📈 Macro signals

- **SBA 7(a) rate UNCHANGED** — Prime 6.75% (Apr 29 FOMC held at 3.5–3.75%, **8-4 dissent**); SBA 7(a) ~9.75% on $1.5M/10yr → debt service ~$235k/yr. June cut probability only 28% — **don't underwrite to lower rates**. ([Fed FOMC](https://www.federalreserve.gov/newsevents/pressreleases/monetary20260429a.htm), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/29/fed-interest-rate-decision-april-2026.html))
- **Seller financing now ~60% of small business acquisitions.** Standard stack: 50–90% senior debt + 5–10% equity + 5–15% seller carry. **The "50/50 standby rule" lets a full-standby seller note cover 50% of the 10% equity injection → out-of-pocket drops to 5%**. Material expansion of reachable price range. ([ClearlyAcquired](https://www.clearlyacquired.com/blog/the-ultimate-guide-to-business-acquisition-financing-in-2026))
- **Multiples — BizBuySell Q1 2026:** average cash-flow multiple **2.7x** (+3% QoQ); manufacturing deal volume +16% YoY but median sale price -23% to $775k and CF -23% to $268k → **manufacturing buyer's market on price, but lower-quality cash flows**. Retail medians up 9%. ([BizBuySell Insight](https://www.bizbuysell.com/insight-report/))
- **Berkeley/Bay Area CRE:** SF leasing strongest since Q2 2018 (3.82M sf Q1 2026, AI-led top 3 deals); Berkeley availability ~16%; Silicon Valley office 16.5%. **Cap-rate Q2 not yet published** — opportunistic office plays still alive but tightening. ([Cushman SF](https://www.cushmanwakefield.com/en/united-states/insights/us-marketbeats/san-francisco-marketbeats))
- **Portugal D7 — €920/mo unchanged.** IFICI (NHR 2.0) confirmed research/innovation only, **does NOT cover foreign pensions** (taxed at standard PT progressive rates). D7 still viable for residency, no tax shelter on passive income. ([Global Citizen Solutions](https://www.globalcitizensolutions.com/portugal-d7-visa/))
- **BRL/USD = 4.9241 (May 7), -0.30% DoD vs. 4.91 May 6.** Slight BRL weakening — **BR arbitrage window stable, not opening**. Cross within noise; no action needed on BR-side deals. ([TradingEconomics](https://tradingeconomics.com/brazil/currency))
