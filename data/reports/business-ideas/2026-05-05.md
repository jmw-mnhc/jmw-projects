## 📈 MACRO SIGNALS (deal-math implications)

- **SBA 7(a) cost of capital:** Prime is 6.75% (held since Dec 10, 2025; 90% odds it stays at June FOMC). Effective rate **9.00–9.50%** on $250k+ acquisition loans. → **Lock variable now, bake 9.25% into base case, pencil one cut later 2026.**
- **SBA 504 (RE-backed):** April 9, 2026 effective debenture rate **5.78% on the 20-yr** — fully **~350bps cheaper than 7(a)** for the CRE portion. → **If a target owns its building, split-finance: 504 the dirt, 7(a) the goodwill, blended cost drops ~150bps.**
- **Multiples (BizBuySell Q1 2026):** Median **2.7x SDE** (+3% YoY); $2M–$5M tier averages **3.65x SDE / 4.09x EBITDA**; owner-independent at **3.5–5.0x** vs. 1.5–2.5x owner-dependent. → **Don't pay above 2.7x for owner-dependent; only stretch to 4x+ for documented owner-independent ops with recurring revenue.**
- **Seller financing post-Jul-2025 SOP:** Min buyer cash **5%**, full-standby seller note can fill the other 5% — but standby is now for **the full life of the SBA loan**, not 24 months. → **Structure as 5% cash + 5% full-standby + optional non-standby second; the standby tail is permanent dead capital — price the discount accordingly.**
- **Portugal nationality law:** Signed **May 3, 2026**; doubles citizenship clock from 5→10 yrs (7 for EU/CPLP). D7/D2 residency rights and 5-yr permanent residency unchanged. **Pending applications grandfathered.** → **PR-after-5-years still works as the EU-flexibility play; if citizenship was the goal, holding period doubled — re-underwrite any PT exit timeline.**
- **Berkeley AGA 2026:** Capped at **1.0%** (65% of 1.5% Bay Area CPI) — structurally below inflation. → **Model 1.0% rent escalators NOT 3% — kills value-add cap-rate compression thesis.**
- **California multifamily Q1 2026:** Cap rate **5.6%**, vacancy **4.8%→5.1% by Q4**; Berkeley studio rents **$1,928 avg** (+1.7% YoY). → **Cap rate compression unlikely until 2027 cuts.**
