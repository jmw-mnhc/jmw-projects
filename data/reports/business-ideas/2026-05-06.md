- **Portugal NHR/D7:** NHR 1.0 fully sunset; **NHR 2.0 / IFICI** is sector-restricted (tech, R&D, exec mgmt) — a generic SMB-acquirer likely won't qualify. **D7 minimum passive income raised to €920/mo (€11,040/yr) Jan 1 2026**. Tax shield meaningfully narrower than 2024. ([D7 details](https://getgoldenvisa.com/portugal-d7-visa))
- **Berkeley CRE:** average cap **~5.91%**, office availability rising to 16% (from 13.4% YoY) — softening favors buyers; owner-user SBA 504 on a distressed office/mixed-use is more realistic than chasing yield. ([Cushman & Wakefield Oakland MarketBeat](https://www.cushmanwakefield.com/en/united-states/insights/us-marketbeats/oakland-marketbeats))
- **BRL/USD: ~4.91 (May 6).** Real has strengthened **+11.8% YTD / +14.5% YoY** — Brazilian SMBs are ~12% more expensive in USD vs January. **Brazil arbitrage window is closing fast.** Lean toward USD-revenue-export businesses if pursuing BR.
